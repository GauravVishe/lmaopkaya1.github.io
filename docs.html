About

Vishal started vIOLENT to learn coding but ended up turning into something absolutely incredible. He released it to the public, and learned from the steady growth that he was in the right direction and that the public had pretty good taste hehe. And so he continued to rock a service people loved and adored which turned into this bot of awesomeness before you! So far it's been a really fun solo project for Vishal and he wishes to share that fun by allowing everyone to invite the bot to your server!

To make it even squeaky clean, the Violent bot has been rewritten for you to have the smoothest time Listening Songs and Vibin with your buddies! And yeah, it'll always stay up to date on those silly API switches!
